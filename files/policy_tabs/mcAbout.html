About Us

WhatSock is based in the San Francisco Bay Area, and was founded by Bryan Garaventa in 2010 to provide enterprise business solutions for the development of performance enhanced, automatically accessible web technologies.

Bryan Garaventa attended Notre Dame de Namur University in Belmont California; worked at Napster in the early 2000's to introduce accessibility within mainstream applications; has worked to expand the concept of Accessible Innovation in subsequent years; is a member of the Guild of Accessible Web Designers, and is a Fellow of the RSA (Royal Society of Arts).

WhatSock provides the AccDC API as a free scalable business solution, which can be integrated with any architecture, platform, or framework that supports JavaScript.

All visual design by Angela Ricci (web designer and web front-end developer). You can check her work at her personal site or you can follow her on Twitter.
